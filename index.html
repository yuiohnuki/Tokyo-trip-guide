TOKYO TRIP GUIDE BY YUI

As, a one who lived in Tokyo for most of my life I will introduce my favorite places in Tokyo!!

Foods

  • Saizeriya

    • italian restaurant where you can full your belly in $5!!

  • shyabuyo

    • hot pot place where it is all you can eat and you can eat whatever you want in $10!!

  • omuretsu monogatari

    • the omuraisu; soft eggs with ketchup rice and it's really soft

    Entertainment

  • karaoke

    • you can sing whatever you want for a few hours in $3!!

    There are bunch of songs! I personally likes Gracie Abrams

  • Purikura

    • Japanese style photobooth which makes your face prettier!
